 Training 

Passion is demonstrated in every training session. At the Ohana Football Sports Centre we like to offer the best to all teams that choose us. This is why our facilities are constantly being improved: from the rooms with the latest equipment to the fields with the hybrid technology.

  • Training for kids from ages 8 to 18.
  • Personalized for the kids, no matter where they are in their sporting journey.
  • Special training packages for those 'kids-at-heart' who are still eager to learn new skills, or take on a new hobby.
  • We offer 1-to-1 trainings, weekly group training and holiday football development camps.
  • All our coaches are qualified and professional football coaches.
