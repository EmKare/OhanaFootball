"Football is life" and we here at the Ohana Football Sports Centre are committed to making this statement a reality. Teams from all over the country, and from all levels, choose the facilities and fields of Ohana Football Sports Centre to train and perfect their technique. We offer training camps, monthly tournaments and training with professionals from the highest level.


  • We offer a wide range of services, including kids' professional football training, and various different playing surfaces for different footballing experiencs.

  • Our pitches are durable, and are in standard with the criteria set by the FIFA™.

  • We have an on-site sports store, fully stocked with top quality sports eqipment for different sporting disciplines.

  • Have a match or a competition to host, or want to play some ball with some friends? Just contact our Rental Services Department to find out more.
