 Rental 

The Ohana Football Sports Centre boasts a number of top-quality pitches with all the necessary equipment approved by both the Jamaica Football Federation and FIFA. At the Ohana Football Sports Centre, we rent our professional fields to organise sporting events tailored to your needs.

  • 6 medium size, artificial grass football fields, and 2 medium natural grass fields, for 7v7 matches.
  • Our medium pitches can be easily converted into a full size pitch.
  • 2 full natural grass fields for 11v11 matches.
  • 2 indoor 7v7 artificial grass pitches, and 2 indoor 7v7 futsal pitches, for football no matter the weather.
  • Adequate and safe parking so everyone can play without worry.
  • We have sanitary changing rooms, fitted with bathroom facilities, for all our fields.
  • We have artificial lights on all our pitches, for nighttime football.
  • Our rental fields are open:
    • Weekdays: 5 pm to 11 pm
    • Weekends: 3 pm to 11 pm
